The Problem

There are billions of inaccessible neurons and over 400 miles of blood vessels that navigate every part of the brain.

The Threat

Damaged neurons can wreak havoc on our bodies and lives.

The Solution

Our team has spent decades studying the application of our technology with an endovascular surgical procedure. We have solved how to deliver electronics into the wall of the blood vessel, giving us access to an unprecedented amount of data from untouched areas of the brain. This will fundamentally change how we study, diagnose, and treat the brain.
